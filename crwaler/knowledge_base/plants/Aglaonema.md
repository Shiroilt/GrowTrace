## Expert Notes

Aglaonema is one of the most tolerant indoor plants and survives lower light conditions better than most tropical foliage plants.

Overwatering is the leading cause of failure.

New shoot emergence is a strong indicator that root development has started successfully.

Stable moisture is more important than excessive watering during propagation.

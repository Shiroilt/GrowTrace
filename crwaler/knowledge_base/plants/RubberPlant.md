## Expert Notes

Rubber Plant roots significantly faster when propagated during warm months.

Air layering generally provides the highest success rate because roots develop before the stem is separated from the parent plant.

A cutting can remain alive for several weeks without rooting, therefore leaf survival alone does not guarantee successful root development.

Monitoring root development indicators together with moisture and temperature trends provides a more reliable rooting prediction than visual observation alone.

# Rose Knowledge Base

* Common Name: Rose
* Scientific Name: Rosa spp.
* Family: Rosaceae
* Genus: Rosa

* Thorn-bearing stems

* Stem Cuttings

## Expert Notes

Rose cuttings root best from semi-hardwood stems.

Rooting hormone significantly improves success rate.

Flower production depends heavily on sunlight availability.

High humidity combined with poor airflow is one of the most common causes of fungal disease in roses.

For cutting propagation, shoot development is often the strongest indicator that root formation has started successfully.

# Hibiscus Knowledge Base

## Plant Identity

* Common Name: Hibiscus
* Other Name: China Rose, Jasud
* Scientific Name: Hibiscus rosa-sinensis
* Family: Malvaceae
* Genus: Hibiscus
* Species: rosa-sinensis
* Plant Type: Flowering Shrub
* Growth Habit: Upright Bushy Shrub
* Life Cycle: Perennial

## Native Region

Native to:

* East Asia
* China

Cultivated in:

* India
* Southeast Asia
* Tropical Regions Worldwide

## Growth Characteristics

* Produces large colorful flowers
* Fast growth in warm climates
* Flowers almost year-round in tropical areas
* Responds strongly to pruning

## Propagation Knowledge

### Propagation Methods

* Stem Cuttings
* Air Layering
* Grafting

### Best Cutting

Contains:

* Semi-hardwood stem
* 10–20 cm length
* 3–5 nodes
* Disease-free tissue

## Root Development Knowledge

### Early Rooting Signs

* Callus formation
* Bud swelling
* Stem remains green

## Expert Notes

Hibiscus is one of the easiest flowering shrubs to propagate from cuttings.

Warm temperatures significantly improve rooting speed.

Flower production is strongly linked to sunlight exposure.

New branch development is a strong indicator that root formation has successfully occurred.

Excess water is the most common cause of cutting failure.

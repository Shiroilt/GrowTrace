## Expert Notes

Tulsi is one of the easiest medicinal plants to propagate from cuttings.

Leaf production rate can be used as a strong indicator of root establishment.

Essential oil production generally increases when sunlight and root health improve.

For your AI project, Tulsi is a very useful plant because changes in moisture, temperature, humidity, and light quickly appear in leaf growth, making it ideal for training virtual sensor models.

The appearance of new leaves within 10–20 days after propagation is one of the strongest indicators that rooting has started successfully.

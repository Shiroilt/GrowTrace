## Expert Notes

Mogra roots best during warm humid months.

Flower production is strongly linked to sunlight exposure.

A healthy rooting cutting typically produces new shoots before visible flowers.

Stable moisture produces better rooting results than heavy daily watering.

Fragrance intensity often increases when plants receive adequate sunlight and healthy root development.

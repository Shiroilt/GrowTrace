## Expert Notes

Curry Leaf plants strongly respond to sunlight and nitrogen availability.

New leaf flushes are one of the best indicators of root establishment.

The species can tolerate drought once established but young cuttings require consistent moisture.

Root suckers can be used as an additional indicator of healthy root system development.

For AI prediction systems, leaf production rate can act as a strong proxy for root health and survival probability.

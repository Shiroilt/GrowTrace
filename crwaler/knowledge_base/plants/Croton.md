## Expert Notes

Croton is highly sensitive to environmental fluctuations.

Sudden movement from one location to another often causes leaf drop even when the plant is healthy.

Bright light is essential for maintaining vibrant foliage colors.

Stable humidity and temperature significantly improve propagation success.

Leaf color intensity can be used as a useful indirect indicator of overall plant health.

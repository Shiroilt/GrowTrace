# Root Formation Biology

## Root Initiation

Definition:

Formation of new roots from stem tissue.

### Requirements

* Oxygen
* Moisture
* Suitable temperature
* Living nodes

### Major Hormone

Auxin

Examples:

* IAA
* IBA
* NAA

### AI Importance

Root initiation is the first biological sign that rooting has started.

---

# Callus Formation

Definition:

Protective tissue formed at wound site.

### Functions

* Prevent infection
* Begin root formation

### Typical Timeline

2–14 Days

### AI Interpretation

Callus present:

Positive rooting sign

---

# Adventitious Root Formation

Definition:

Roots formed from stems instead of existing roots.

### Found In

* Money Plant
* Monstera
* Syngonium
* Rose
* Hibiscus

### AI Importance

Most cutting propagation depends on adventitious roots.

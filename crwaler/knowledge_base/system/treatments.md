# Plant Treatment Knowledge Base

## Root Rot

### Symptoms

* Yellow leaves
* Black roots
* Mushy stem
* Bad smell from soil
* Wilting despite wet soil

### Immediate Action

* Stop watering
* Remove plant from pot
* Inspect roots

### Treatment

* Cut all black roots
* Keep only healthy white roots
* Sterilize scissors before cutting
* Repot into fresh well-draining soil

### Recovery Time

14–60 Days

### Prevention

* Improve drainage
* Reduce watering frequency
* Use aerated soil

---

## Overwatering

### Symptoms

* Yellow leaves
* Soft stem
* Wet soil for many days
* Leaf drop

### Immediate Action

* Stop watering

### Treatment

* Allow soil to dry
* Increase airflow
* Check drainage holes

### Recovery Time

7–30 Days

### Prevention

* Water only when required
* Use moisture sensor

---
